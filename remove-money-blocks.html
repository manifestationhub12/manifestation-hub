Money Mindset

How to Remove
Money Blocks Forever

✦ Manifestation Hub · 8 min read

You can do every manifestation technique perfectly — but if you have hidden money blocks, abundance will always slip through your fingers. Here's how to find and clear them.

A money block is a subconscious belief that repels wealth. You don't choose these beliefs — you absorb them from childhood, culture, and past experiences. They run quietly in the background, sabotaging every effort to build financial freedom.

You cannot out-manifest a limiting belief. If part of you believes money is bad, no amount of affirmations will fix it. You have to clear the block first.

The 5 Most Common Money Blocks

Block 1
"Money is the root of all evil."

You were taught that wealthy people are greedy or dishonest. So your subconscious protects you from becoming "one of them" — by keeping you broke.

Block 2
"I don't deserve wealth."

Somewhere, you decided you're not worthy of abundance. Maybe from criticism, comparison, or failure. So when money comes, you unconsciously push it away.

Block 3
"Money changes people."

You fear that becoming wealthy will change who you are — or drive people away. So you stay "safe" by staying where you are financially.

Block 4
"You have to work hard for every penny."

You believe money only comes through suffering. So when opportunities arrive easily, you distrust them — and sabotage them.

Block 5
"There's never enough."

A scarcity mindset rooted in past hardship. You believe money is limited, so you hoard, stress, and block the flow of abundance.

1Identify Your Blocks

Read the five blocks above again. Which ones made your stomach tighten? Which ones felt uncomfortably true? That reaction is your subconscious telling you something. Write them down. Awareness is the first step to clearing.

2Question the Belief

For each block, ask: Where did this belief come from? Is it actually true? Whose voice is it — mine, or someone else's? Most money blocks are inherited, not chosen. Recognizing that they were given to you makes it easier to let them go.

3Reframe It

Replace each limiting belief with an empowering one:

  • "Money is the root of all evil" → "Money is a tool for good. I use it to help myself and others."
  • "I don't deserve wealth" → "I am worthy of abundance simply because I exist."
  • "There's never enough" → "The universe is abundant. There is more than enough for everyone."

4Feel the New Belief

This is where most people fail. They write the new belief but don't feel it. Your subconscious responds to emotion, not logic. As you say your new belief, imagine how your life would feel if it were true. Feel the relief, the freedom, the peace. That feeling is what reprograms the mind.

5Reinforce Daily

Clearing money blocks isn't a one-time event. Old beliefs creep back. Spend two minutes each morning repeating your reframed beliefs with feeling. Over time, the new belief becomes your default.

Final Thoughts

Money blocks are not your fault — but clearing them is your responsibility. The good news: beliefs are not permanent. They're habits of thought. And like any habit, they can be changed with awareness and consistency.

Start today. Identify one block. Reframe it. Feel the new truth. Repeat daily. Your financial reality will follow your beliefs. ✦
