Money Mindset

Remove Money Blocks Forever

✦ Manifestation Hub

You can do every manifestation technique perfectly — but if you have hidden money blocks, abundance will always slip away.

A money block is a subconscious belief that repels wealth. You absorb them from childhood and culture.

You cannot out-manifest a limiting belief. Clear the block first.

1The 5 Most Common Blocks

  • 'Money is evil' — wealthy people seen as greedy
  • 'I don't deserve wealth' — feeling unworthy
  • 'Money changes people' — fear of becoming different
  • 'Must work hard for every penny' — distrust easy money
  • 'There's never enough' — scarcity mindset

2Identify Your Blocks

Which ones made your stomach tighten? That reaction is your subconscious. Write them down.

3Reframe

Replace: 'Money is evil' → 'Money is a tool for good.'

4Feel the New Belief

Your subconscious responds to emotion, not logic. Feel the relief as you say the new belief.
